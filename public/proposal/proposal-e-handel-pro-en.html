An e-commerce platform built for wholesale and B2B in furniture and building materials: thousands of SKUs, customer-specific pricing and live stock straight from your ERP. Your resellers order themselves, around the clock — and your team is freed from manual orders.

For large catalogs & ERP integration
The problem

Manual order handling is capping your growth

Orders by email and phone

Your reps enter orders by hand for every reseller. It eats time, creates errors and simply doesn't scale.

Prices and stock are out of sync

Without an ERP link, the wrong price and the wrong stock level show up — and every customer has their own contract pricing.

A heavy catalog to manage

Thousands of SKUs across several markets and currencies are next to impossible to run in an off-the-shelf webshop.

The solution

A B2B platform that works against your ERP

  • Custom platform in React — built for large catalogs, fast search and your specific business logic.
  • Real-time integration with Fortnox/Visma — products, prices, stock and orders sync automatically.
  • Customer-specific B2B pricing across markets and currencies — every reseller sees their own terms.
